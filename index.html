Thinking Outside the fox is a site dedicated to spreading information about the domesticated fox and how this animal fits into our modern day world.

Domesticated foxes are the result of many years of selective breeding to make them more inclined to seek human companionship. While the process still has a way to go, the prospect of having a pet fox today is much more feasible than it was even 50 years ago. Before making the major decision of adopting this animal into your home it is important to have the information that will help you on your journey.

Click the History panel to learn more about how foxes have gotten to where they are today, and click the Breeds and Info/Care panel to learn more about the specific requirement of adopting a fox friend into your own home.
